## Calculations


### 1. Annual global forest loss
- ~10.9 million hectares per year


### 2. Seconds in a year
- 365 × 24 × 60 × 60 = **31,536,000 seconds**


### 3. Forest loss per second
- 10,900,000 hectares ÷ 31,536,000 seconds ≈ **0.3459 hectares per second**


### 4. Convert hectares to football pitches
- 1 football pitch ≈ 0.714 hectares
- 0.3459 hectares ÷ 0.714 hectares per pitch ≈ **0.485 football pitches per second**
